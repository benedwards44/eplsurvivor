EPL Survivor!

Welcome to EPL Survivor! Join groups, create your own and play with friends.

EPL Survivor is a tipping competition based around the English Premier League. The rules are simple, choose one team to win or draw each week. If your team loses, you're out! Outlast all other players and you win the entire prize pool of your group.

Once you've picked a team for a week, you can no longer choose that team for any following weeks. So make your picks wisely.

Head over to the rules page to see a full list of rules.
